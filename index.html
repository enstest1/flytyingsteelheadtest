Introduction

Welcome to our website on steelhead fly tying! Here you will find information and resources on how to tie your own steelhead flies.

Materials

To tie steelhead flies, you will need the following materials:

  • Hook
  • Thread
  • Fur or feathers
  • Flash
  • Eyes

How to Tie

Follow these steps to tie your own steelhead fly:

  1. Start by attaching the hook to your vice.
  2. Tie on your thread and add any underbody materials, such as fur or feathers.
  3. Add flash or other decorative materials, if desired.
  4. Add eyes to the fly, using glue or thread to secure them in place.
  5. Finish the fly by forming a head and trimming any excess materials.
